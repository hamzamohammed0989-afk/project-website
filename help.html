How to Use This Site

Tilmaamaha hoose ayaa kaa caawin doona sida aad u dhex maraysid bogagga, foomamka, sawirrada iyo shaxda.

Step by Step

1. Navigating the Site

Isticmaal xarriiqda kore (navigation bar) ee ku yaal bogga kasta si aad ugu boodo Home, Gallery, Booking, Help, iyo About. Boggu wuxuu ku hagayaa kaliya sida ay u kala socdaan bogagga — bogga aad hadda ku sugan tahay wuxuu ku muuqan doonaa midab gaar ah (Green).

2. Viewing the Gallery & Table

Bogga Gallery wuxuu ka kooban yahay sawirro iyo shax (table) muujinaya qiimaha menu-ga. Waxaad ku dhex socon kartaa xariiqaha table-ka si aad u aragto qiimayaasha kala duwan.

3. Filling the Booking Form

  1. Geli magacaaga oo dhan iyo major-kaaga.
  2. Dooro jinsigaaga (radio button) iyo hobbies-kaaga (checkbox — waxaad dooran kartaa mid ka badan).
  3. Dooro taariikhda, saacadda, iyo tirada dadka.
  4. Haddii aad qorto faallo dheeraad ah, isticmaal box-ka Comments.
  5. Riix Confirm reservation — foomku wuxuu ku baaraan doonaa xogta si sax ah (tusaale ahaan magaca inuusan madhnaan) ka hor inta uusan la gudbin.
